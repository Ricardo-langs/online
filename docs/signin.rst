ufile sign in
=============

Signing in to your UFile account allows you to securely access your tax data, manage returns, and continue your work from any device. Follow these steps to log in بسهولة and safely.

Steps to Sign in Ufile
=========

Step 1 - Open your web browser  
Launch your browser and ensure you have internet access.

Step 2 - Visit the UFile sign-in page  
Enter the official UFile website and navigate to the login section.

Step 3 - Enter your credentials  
Provide your registered email and password.

Step 4 - Click on sign in  
Press the sign-in button to access your account.

Step 5 - Verify your identity  
Complete any additional verification if required.

Step 6 - Access your dashboard  
Once logged in, you can manage your account and files.

Step 7 - Keep your account secure  
Always log out after use, especially on shared devices.
